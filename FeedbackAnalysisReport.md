**Suggestion:** The `isRDSMagic` guard in `validateFileMagic` (DataStudio.jsx:371) checks only for XDR (`X\n` = 0x58 0x0A), ASCII (`A\n`), native binary (`B\n`), and gzip (0x1F 0x8B).
